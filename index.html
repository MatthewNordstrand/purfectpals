Adopt, Don't Shop!

Shopping for a pet are both bad for the environment and is bad for the animals involved. It increases stress on the animal shelter system, and also makes it more difficult to be a no-kill shelter. Adopting a pet will give one a home that otherwise wasn't in a loving home. Please consider adopting before you think about shopping for a pet.
